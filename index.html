Welcome to Lia Bank

Our Mission

The vision at LiaBank is to empower individuals and businesses through access to secure, inclusive, and modern financial solutions. We commit to long-lasting relations through personalized services and business ethics, besides caring for the financial growth and stability of our clients. We believe in the focus on hi-tech and customer satisfaction as the way to simplify banking for all of our customers.

Our Services

At LiaBank, we provide a wide range of financial services that seek to meet the needs of individuals and corporate customers. From personal banking, including savings and checking accounts, we offer special loan solutions to make banking easy and accessible. To our business customers, we can also provide special services including business loans, payroll management, and investment advisory services to foster growth and stability. LiaBank puts all your banking needs at your fingertips with our digital banking platform, which is accessible and secure anywhere, anytime.

Customer Support

At LiaBank, we prioritize customer satisfaction and are dedicated to providing exceptional support. For any question or inquiry, feel free to reach our Customer Support team. Have immediate answers with our Help Center, full of FAQs and guides. Whether you reach us on the phone, via email, or through online chat, knowledgeable representatives stand ready to make your banking experience smooth. We take your needs very seriously and are committed to guiding and assisting you at every step.

Security Information

At LiaBank, data security and privacy of our customers have always been foremost. Our digital banking platform employs advanced security protocols and encryption for the protection of your personal and financial information against any unauthorized access. Your accounts are secure through multi-factor authentication, secure login methods, and regular system audits. We have a specific security operations team that monitors suspicious activities on a continuous basis and responds swiftly to various threats. Moreover, we advise our clients to be more observant with account credentials, not to share personal information, and to log in to LiaBank only through official channels. Your trust is our responsibility, and we are going to take good care of your information at all times.
